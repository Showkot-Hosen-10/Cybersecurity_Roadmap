## 1Ô∏è‚É£ What is Cybersecurity?

**Cybersecurity** is the practice of protecting **systems, networks, devices, and data** from cyber attacks such as hacking, malware, phishing, and data breaches.

It involves:

* Network security
* System & OS security
* Application & web security
* Cryptography & information security
* Incident detection and response

Cybersecurity ensures **confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA triad)** of information.

### (iii) Future Scope of Cybersecurity (Will AI Kill This Field?)

**Short answer: NO.**

* AI is **increasing attacks**, not reducing them
* AI itself needs **secure systems, models, and data**
* New domains are emerging:

  * AI Security
  * Cloud Security
  * IoT & Critical Infrastructure Security

Cybersecurity will **evolve**, not die. It is more stable than many programming-only roles.
